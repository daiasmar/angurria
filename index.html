• Angurria is played with two standard six-sided dice and has 10 rounds.
  • Each player takes turns in each round rolling the two dice and accumulating points.
  • At the beginning of each turn, the player rolls two dice and adds up the numbers to determine their score for that turn.
  • The player can choose to roll the dice as many times as they want during their turn , and the sum of the two dice is added to their score each time they roll.
  • However, if the player rolls a 7 during their turn, they immediately lose their turn and all the points accumulated during that turn.
  • The player can choose to end their turn at any time before rolling a 7 to keep the points accumulated up to the next turn.
  • The player with the most points at the end of the 10 rounds is declared the winner.
